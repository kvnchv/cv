About me

I am a graduate research assistant pursuing my PhD in Materials Science under Dr. David L. McDowell at the Georgia Institute of Technology. My expertise lies in molecular dynamics and scientific software development. My research focuses primarily on physics-based atomistic and coarse-grained atomistic modeling of multicomponent alloys and fundamental deformation processes at the nanoscale.

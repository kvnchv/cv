About

I am a graduate research assistant pursuing my PhD in Materials Science under Dr. David L. McDowell at the Georgia Institute of Technology. My expertiee lies in physics-based atomistic and coarse-grained atomistic modeling of multicomponent alloys and fundamental deformation processes at the nanoscale.
